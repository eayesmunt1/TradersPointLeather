Our Philosophy

Every stitch tells a story of precision, quality, and craftsmanship.

At Traders Point Leather, we care about getting the details right. Each piece is carefully cut from quality leathers — including Horween and Wickett & Craig — precisely stitched with heavy-duty bonded nylon thread, and hand-burnished to a smooth, polished edge finish. No shortcuts. No cheap materials. Just exceptional craftsmanship from central Indiana.

01

Premium Leather

We source quality full-grain leathers — including Horween and Wickett & Craig — many of which develop a rich pull-up effect that deepens and patinas with the use of the owner. Each piece gets better with age.

02

Precision Stitched

Every seam is stitched with bonded nylon or bonded polyester thread — the same materials used in heavy-duty industrial applications. Clean, consistent, and incredibly strong.

03

Hand Burnished

Every piece has its edges hand-burnished to a glossy, polished finish. It's a slow process — but it's what separates a handcrafted piece from a factory one.
